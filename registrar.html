About the Registrar's Office

The Office of the Registrar at **PHILLIPINE STATE UNIVERSITY** is responsible for maintaining accurate academic records and supporting students' academic progress. It manages student enrollment, grades, transcripts, and certification requests, ensuring that all records comply with institutional standards.

The office also facilitates the registration process, oversees graduation requirements, and assists with credential verification. Committed to delivering excellent service, the Registrar's Office plays a vital role in student and faculty support by providing efficient administrative services and safeguarding academic integrity. Its focus is on ensuring a seamless academic experience for the entire PSU community.
