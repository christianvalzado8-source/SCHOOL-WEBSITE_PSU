APPLY TO PSU

Your journey to becoming a part of the Phillipine State University community starts here. Follow the steps below for a smooth application process.

Undergraduate Application Process

**Important:** The application period for AY 2025-2026 is from **October 15, 2024 to January 30, 2025**. Late applications will not be processed.
1

Submit Online Application Form

Fill out the official PSU Online Application Form. Ensure all information is accurate and matches your supporting documents.

  • Prepare a **digital copy** of your latest ID photo (2x2 with white background).
  • Choose your preferred degree programs (up to 3 choices).
  • Pay the **P500** non-refundable application fee online or at the Cashier's Office.
2

Take the PSU Entrance Exam (PSUET)

Upon successful submission, you will receive an Examination Permit via email. Follow the instructions for your scheduled exam date and location.

  • Bring your **printed Examination Permit** and **valid ID** (e.g., School ID, National ID).
  • The exam covers English, Math, Science, and Abstract Reasoning.
3

Check Results and Interview

The list of successful examinees will be posted on the PSU Admissions Portal.

  • **Shortlisted applicants** must attend a mandatory interview with the College Dean/Department Head.
  • Failure to attend the interview will forfeit your slot.
4

Enrollment and Document Submission

Once accepted, you must submit all hard-copy documents to the Admissions Office to confirm your slot for the upcoming semester.

  • Original Report Card (F138)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • PSA Birth Certificate (Photocopy)
  • Medical Clearance from PSU Clinic
